Chicken Teriyaki


Description

Chicken Teriyaki is a popular Japanese dish made with grilled or pan-cooked chicken glazed in a sweet and savory teriyaki sauce, typically made from soy sauce, mirin, and sugar. It's often served with steamed rice and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Sauce & Seasoning
  • Chicken
  • ginger
  • garlic

Steps

  1. Whisk soy sauce, sake, vinegar, and brown sugar. Add sesame seeds and crushed red pepper.
  2. Heat oils in skillet over high heat.
  3. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook chicken until browned, flip, cook 1 more minute, then remove.
  5. Reduce heat to medium, add ginger and garlic, cook until fragrant.
  6. Add most of the green onions (reserve some for garnish), cook 1 minute.
  7. Add sauce to pan, scrape browned bits, bring to boil, reduce slightly.
  8. Return chicken to pan, stir until coated and cooked through.
  9. Serve over rice, garnish with reserved green onions.
