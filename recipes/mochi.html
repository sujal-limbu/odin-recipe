Mochi


A mochi muffin is a chewy, buttery, and slightly crispy baked treat made with glutinous rice flour. It combines the soft, elastic texture of mochi with the shape and richness of a muffin, often featuring flavors like coconut, brown butter, or matcha

Ingredients

  • Sweet rice flour:
  • Sugars
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Coconut milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Maple syrup

Steps

  1. Whisk the dry ingredients together in one bowl and the wet ingredients in another
  2. Add the dry mixture to the wet mixture
  3. Spoon the batter into prepared muffin cups and sprinkle with sesame seeds
  4. Bake the muffins until a toothpick comes out clean
