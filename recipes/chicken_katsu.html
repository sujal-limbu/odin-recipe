Chicken Katsu


Description

Chicken Katsu is a Japanese dish made of breaded and deep-fried chicken cutlets. The chicken is typically coated in flour, dipped in egg, and covered with crispy panko breadcrumbs before frying


Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Seasonings
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Panko
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Season the chicken, then dredge in flour.
  2. Coat each breast in egg, then press into the Panko
  3. Fry the chicken katsu until golden brown.
